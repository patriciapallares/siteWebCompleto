Erasmus+ Program:

The Erasmus+ program is a unique opportunity for our students and teachers. We offer exchange experiences educational in collaboration with institutions around the world. This initiative promotes multicultural learning, understanding global and personal enrichment.

Erasmus+ is a European Union (EU) program that aims to main objective to promote education, training, youth and sport in Europe. This program offers opportunities for mobility and cooperation between institutions educational, youth and sports organizations, as well as also among students, teaching staff, young people and athletes.

Erasmus+ supports a wide range of activities, including student exchanges, internships, study programs in abroad, cooperation projects between institutions educational, volunteer programs, training projects for teaching staff, initiatives to improve the quality of teaching education and much more.

The program aims to promote mobility transnational, intercultural learning, the development of skills, social inclusion, innovation and cooperation international in the educational and youth field. Erasmus+ is aimed at both young people and professionals in the field of education, training, youth and sports.

Over the years, Erasmus+ has provided opportunities to millions of people to live experiences abroad, improve your language skills, learn about new cultures and enrich your personal and professional development.
